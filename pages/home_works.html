akina speed stars

The Akina SpeedStars are a Gunma-based team led by Koichiro Iketani. Their home course is Akina Pass. They are an amateur level team, with lower skill than most other teams.

red suns

The Akagi RedSuns was a highly skilled team led by Ryosuke Takahashi. Their home course was Akagi, and they were considered to be the fastest team in Gunma.

night kids

The Myogi NightKids is a racing team composed of highly skilled drivers, led by Takeshi Nakazato. Even though the team is united under Takeshi, there seem to be two factions fighting for leadership aligned with Takeshi and the team's number two driver, Shingo Shoji.

impact blue

Impact Blue was a partnership between its two members, Mako Sato and Sayuki. Their home course is Usui Pass. It is revealed that Impact Blue is disbanded in Extra Stage 2 due to Mako leaving for Tokyo to race there for a year.

emperor

Emperor is a highly skilled team lead by Kyoichi Sudo. The entire team is made of Mitsubishi Lancer Evolutions, claiming their cars are the king of the mountains.

project d

Project D was a racing team created by Ryosuke Takahashi with the purpose of taking the best drivers in the Gunma Area, and traveling to challenge other teams in various prefectures.

purple shadow

Purple Shadow is an Ibaraki based team, led by two legendary racers: Dr. Toshiya "God Arm" Joshima and Kozo "God Foot" Hoshino.

seven star leaf

Seven Star Leaf is an average skilled team, and the first team shown facing Project D, although a few prior opponents are shown in Fourth Stage. Based in Tochigi, their home course is the Nichien-Momiji line

spiral

Team Spiral is a Nanamagari based team, and the 3rd line in the "4 lines of defence" strategy formulated by team Sidewinder.

sidewinder

Sidewinder is a highly skilled team that organized the "4 lines of defense", who make their debut in Fifth Stage. Their home course is the Tsubaki Line.

thunder fire

Not much is known about team Thunder Fire and where their home course is. Their symbol is a lightning bolt with the word 'Thunders' written within it.

todo school

The Todo School is a racing school run by a performance shop owner/ex-rally driver named Todo. They are very highly skilled, with many of their graduates going semi-pro/professional. Interestingly, they prefer FWD cars, particularly Hondas, using the Civic Type R as their best "demonstration" car.
